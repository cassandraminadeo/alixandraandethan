Our Story

Alixandra and Ethan began dating April 26, 2019 while they were both in their undergraduate studies. While facing the challenges and difficulties of school they were able to flourish and fall in love. Once they both graduated, they moved from Georgia to Washington, DC and own a beautiful condo together with their adorable tuxedo cat, Wednesday, in the middle of Dupont Circle.

They then got engaged on September 4th, 2023 when they traveled to Copenhagen. A beautiful vacation turned into the most romantic proposal. They are so thrilled to start this next chapter and cannot wait to share their love with everyone!
